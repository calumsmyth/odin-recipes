Spaghetti Bolognese

Description

On this page we will learn to make the classic Italian meal of Spaghetti Bolognese.

A treat for anyone who tastes this recipe. Here we will learn how to make this classic in a simple and inexpensive way.

Ingredients

  • 1 tbsp olive oil
  • 2 rashers smoked streaky bacon, finely chopped
  • 2 medium onions, finely chopped
  • 2 carrots, trimmed and finely chopped
  • 2 celery sticks, finely chopped
  • 2 garlic cloves, finely chopped
  • 2-3 sprigs rosemary, leaves picked and finely chopped
  • 500g beef mince
For the bolognese sauce
  • 2x 400g tins of plum tomatoes
  • Small pack basil leaves picked, 3/4 finely chopped and the rest left whole for garnish
  • 1 tsp dried oregano
  • 2 fresh bay leaves
  • 2 tbsp tomato puree
  • 1 beef stock cube
  • 1 red chilli deseeded and finely chopped (Optional)
  • 125ml red wine
  • 6 cherry tomatoes sliced in half
To season and serve
  • 75g parmesan grated, plus extra to serve
  • 400g spaghetti
  • Crusty bread to serve (Optional)

Steps

  1. Put a large saucepan on medium heat and add 1 tbsp of olive oil
  2. Add 4 finely chopped bacon rashers and fry for 10 mins until golden and crisp
  3. Reduce heat and add 2 onions, 2 carrots, 2 celery sticks, 2 garlic cloves and the leaves of 2-3 sprigs rosemary, all finely chopped. Fry for 10 mins. Stir the veg often until soft.
  4. Increase to medium-high heat. Add 500g beef mince and cook, stirring for 3-4 mins until meat is browned all over.
  5. Add 2 tins of plum tomatoes, the finely chopped leaves from 3/4 small pack of basil, 1 tsp dried oregano, 2 bay leaves, 2 tbsp tomatoe puree, 1 beef stock cube, 1 deseeded and chopped red chilli (if using), 125ml red wine and 6 halved cherry tomatoes. Stir with a wooden spoon, breaking up the plum tomatoes
  6. Bring to the boil, reduce to a gentle simmer and cover with lid. Cook for 1 hr 15 mins, occasionally stirring until rich, thick sauce.
  7. Add 75g grated parmesan, check seasoning and stir
  8. When bolognese is nearly finished, cook 400g of spaghetti, following instructions on packet
  9. Drain spaghetti and either stir in the bolognese sauce or serve the sauce on top. Serve with additional grated parmesan, the remaining basil leaves and crusty bread
