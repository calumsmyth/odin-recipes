Pizza

Description

On this page we will look at the best way to make your own delicious pizza at home!

Without breaking the bank, we will explain in very simple steps, how you too can make a mouth watering pizza for yourself.

If you too want to know the secrets to pizza success, take a look at our simple guide below.

Ingredients

For the base
  • 300g Strong bread flour
  • 1 tbsp instant yeast
  • 1 tbsp salt
  • 1 tbsp olive oil
For the tomato sauce
  • 100ml passata
  • handful of fresh basil or 1 tsp dried
  • 1 garlic clove
For the Topping
  • 125g ball mozzarella
  • Grated or shaved parmsan
  • Handful of cherry tomatoes

Steps

  1. Roll out the dough and knead it. Then split it into two balls
  2. On a floured surface, roll out the dough into large circles, about 25cm across using a rolling pin
  3. Ensure dough is very thin as it will rise. Lift these dough circles onto two floured baking sheets
  4. Heat oven to 240C/220 fan/ gas 8
  5. Put another baking sheet or an upturned baking tray in the oven on the top shelf
  6. Smooth sauce over bases with back of spoon
  7. Scatter cheese and tomatoes, drizzle olive oil and season
  8. Put one pizza, still on its baking sheet, on top of the preheated sheet or tray and back for 8-10 mins until crisp
  9. Serve with extra olive oil.
  10. Repeat above steps for second pizza
