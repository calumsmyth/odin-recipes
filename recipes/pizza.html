Pizza

Description

On this page we will look at the best way to make your own delicious pizza at home!

Without breaking the bank, we will explain in very simple steps, how you too can make a mouth watering pizza for yourself.

If you too want to know the secrets to pizza success, take a look at our simple guide below.

Ingredients

For the base
  • 300g Strong bread flour
  • 1 tbsp instant yeast
  • 1 tbsp salt
  • 1 tbsp olive oil
For the tomato sauce
  • 100ml passata
  • handful of fresh basil or 1 tsp dried
  • 1 garlic clove
For the Topping
  • 125g ball mozzarella
  • Grated or shaved parmsan
  • Handful of cherry tomatoes
